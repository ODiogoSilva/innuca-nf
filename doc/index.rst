INNUca-NF documentation!
========================

INNUca-NF is a NextFlow_ implementation of the `INNUENDO pipeline`_ designed
for the high quality assembly, quality control and annotation of
bacterial genomes. As a NextFlow pipeline, it can be easily deployed in
many computing environments with minimal requirements.

Requirements
------------

    - `Java 8`_
    - Docker_
    - NextFlow_


.. _INNUENDO pipeline: https://github.com/B-UMMI/INNUca
.. _Docker: https://www.docker.com/
.. _Java 8: https://java.com/en/download/
.. _NextFlow: https://www.nextflow.io/
